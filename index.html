BMW 5

The 2020 BMW 5 Series carries a Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (MSRP) starting at $54,895, including the $995 destination charge. The 530e plug-in hybrid costs the same and we applaud BMW for not charging extra. The 540i starts at $60,445. All-wheel drive in these models is an extra $2,300.
